This dense neighborhood boasts a mix of all kinds of intense combat. Those looking for CQC will be on ground, weaving in and out of each building,Expect to find King of the Hill style gameplay

Fight Night

Pathfinder’s Fight Night is an oval amphitheatre with a boxing ring at its center. Entering the ring will prevent any player from using their weapons and any of their abilities.

Oasis

Oasis is unique due to the choice to fight above ground or below, where the luxurious Oasis Cafe can be found. Since most of the loot is focused inside, expect fights to break out between the buildings.
